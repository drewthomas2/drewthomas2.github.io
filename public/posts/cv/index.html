Senior Design Project#

Cal Poly - San Luis Obispo, CA

Sept. 2023 - Jun. 2024

Along with a small team, created a Thermo-Electric Generator (TEG) module optimized to convert heat into electricity for people living in remote areas. The device is designed to be mounted to a wood burning stove and drive a 5V and 12V supply to charge a battery. A maximum power point tracking (MPPT) IC and several DC-DC switching regulators were used to convert the unregulated supply from the TEG into useable power. I assisted with board layout, circuit design, test design, and microcontroller coding. Our team completed a thesis paper on the project which is available on Cal Poly Digital Commons.
